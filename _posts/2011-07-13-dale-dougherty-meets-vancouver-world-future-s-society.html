--- layout: post title: Dale Dougherty Meets Vancouver @ World Future's Society published: true ---
This past weekend, Vancouver was fortunate enough to be graced by the presence of Dale Dougherty, one of the co-founders of O'Reilly Media and the founder and publisher of O'Reilly's Make Magazine. He is an awesome guy, and I have to say that the talk he gave at the World Future's Society inspired me so much, my optimism for our future has been revitalized to Pre-space-shuttle-retirement levels. He reminded me that the future is what you make of it, so hurry up an start Making!@#!
